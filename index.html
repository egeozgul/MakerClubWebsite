Our Mission

We Imagine, Design, and Print.

In MakerClub an idea brainstormed and developed. Then it is converted to 3d models and circuit board blueprints using CAD programs. Then prototypes are tested, and after finalizing the design, mass production is performed via local companies. Once the prototype design is done, our own 3D printers are used to build a prototype. Finally it is time to sell our products.

This is how we run the MakerClub and establish our start-up experience.

For New Inventors...

If you don't have any experience using 3D printers, CAD programs, circuits, and coding but you are super interested in these areas, you are welcome to give it a try in MakerClub.
