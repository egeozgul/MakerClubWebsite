Our Mission

We Imagine, Design, and Print. Once an idea is concepualized, we use CAD programs such as SolidWorks to 3D design and Altium to design the circuit boards. After the designs are completed, we run our 3D printers to prototype. Once we are done with them, we outsource the mass production lines. It is time to sell our products around the school. This is how our club runs, this is basically our start-up experience. We utilize CAD softwares (SolidWorks, OnShape, 3DSmax(exception:))) to design. Once we finalize the design, we prepare them to be 3D printed and let the two 3d printers do the rest of the job for us. In this way, we can produce even the most complex parts using 3D printers.
