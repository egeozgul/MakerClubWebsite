Fridge Opener

Developers: Egemen Ertem, Ege Özgül, Kaya Alp Türer





Imagine you have a heavy dish of meal in your hand which you want to put it into the refrigerator. Because the meal is heavy, you cannot hold it in one hand. You want to open the refrigerator's door while holding the dish. Imagine yourself jumping on one leg and putting the dish on the other one and also holding the dish with one hand, you are trying to open the refrigerator door with your other hand. Or, you could use this device to open the refrigerator's door by only pressed this pedal. If your arm is broken, this thing can save a help a lot.






The special thing about this pedal is that its parts come out of the 3D printer already assembled. So all parts can be printed in a single shot.




Finally we took the first place and won a zaxe 3d printer which we still use it.
